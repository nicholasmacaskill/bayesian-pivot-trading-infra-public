# Strategy 3: ICT Order Flow (Institutional Sponsorship)

## Core Philosophy
This strategy focuses on **Market Structure Shifts (MSS)** and the **Order Blocks (OB)** that originate them. It assumes that when institutions reverse a trend or continue a strong move, they leave a footprint at the origin of the displacement. We seek to enter when price returns to mitigate this block.

## Components
### 1. Market Structure Shift (MSS)
- **Definition**: Price creates a Swing High/Low that breaks the previous opposing Swing Point with **Displacement**.
- **Displacement**: Fast, energetic move (large candles, gaps/FVGs).
- **Trend Change**: If we were making Lower Lows, and we suddenly break the last Lower High with displacement, that is a Bullish MSS.

### 2. Order Block (OB)
- **Bullish OB**: The last *down-close* candle (or series of down candles) immediately BEFORE the displacement up.
- **Bearish OB**: The last *up-close* candle (or series of up candles) immediately BEFORE the displacement down.
- **Validation**: The move away from the OB *must* create a Fair Value Gap (FVG). This proves intent.

### 3. Entry Model
- **Trigger**: Price retraces into the Order Block / FVG combination.
- **Entry Price**: 
  - Aggressive: High/Low of the OB body.
  - Conservative: Mean Threshold (50% of the OB body).
- **Stop Loss**: Just beyond the OB (plus buffer).
- **Target**: Next Liquidity Pool (Old Highs/Lows) or 1:3 RR.

## Rules
1. **Bias Check**: Must align with the 4H/Daily Bias (Multi-Factor Model).
2. **Time**: Setup must form or trigger during Killzones (London/NY).
3. **Sponsorship**: The displacement leg must have volume/FVG.
